About Lockify

A privacy-first app lock built for Android users who value security without complexity.

What is Lockify?

Lockify is a secure, lightweight, and smooth app lock for Android. It lets you protect any app on your device with a PIN, pattern, password, or biometric authentication — ensuring that only you can access your private apps.

Unlike many app lockers that are bloated with ads and unnecessary features, Lockify is focused, fast, and privacy-respecting. Your credentials and app data never leave your device.

Whether you share your phone with family, want to protect sensitive apps, or simply value your privacy — Lockify gives you reliable protection with zero compromise on performance.

Our Mission

Focused Purpose

Lockify does one thing and does it well — protecting your apps. No bloat, no distractions, no unnecessary permissions.
